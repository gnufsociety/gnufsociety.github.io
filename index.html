What is it

Something you've never seen before. And you are the main character.


The Application

OpenChallenge is a new app that allows you to challenge users in anything that comes to your mind: Food&Drink Challenges, Physical Challenges, Ability Challenges... and in whatever you want to test your skills.
Just Register, Create or Find a Challenge and go to show who's the best by winning prizes and much more!
The only requirement is TO HAVE FUN.

The Human Network

To describe Open Challenge we coined the new term of Human Network.
Users are called to meet in places to take part in challenges: here people are not passive and can put themselves to the test or just have fun with close friends.
The main differences with a Social Network is that with Open Challenge people interact each other with something more than some photos or comments: users share not just their passions but they fully put themselves in game.

The Focus

Pubs and coffee shops are an important part of the atmosphere and are especially encouraged to organize challenges in order to increase te number of clients of their activites.
No more boring friday nights! According to pub's owners you can really create unforgettable moments for you, your friends and for all the challengers!
