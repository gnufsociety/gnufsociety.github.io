How did the team born

The GNUF Team was officially formed during the Google Workshop 2016 with the main purpose to create a new application to launch on the market.
However we were calssmates during the first three years in Computer Engineering, so we decided to take part in this Challenge to enjoy new experiences and to learn something new. And now we're here, working for you to improve your user-experience in this project made for you that we decided to call Open Challenge.
