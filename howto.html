    How to get Open Challenge

    Just follow our instructions: you can't miss it!

    First, set your smartphone

  • Go on settings, than tap on security
  • Turn on Unknown sources (easy bro, it's nothing dangerous)

    • Download the application

  • Scroll down until you reach the download button
  • Tap on it and select an account for the direct download from Google Drive
  • Once downloaded, tap on install and wait for it

    • Enjoy!

  • The app is ready: register and have fun!
